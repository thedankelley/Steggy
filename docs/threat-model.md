# Steggy Threat Model Presets

Steggy does not assume a single adversary.

Users face different risks depending on geography, role, and urgency.
Threat model presets provide safe defaults without removing control.

---

## Journalist

Focus: Confidentiality + metadata hygiene  
Primary risks: seizure, passive surveillance

---

## Activist

Focus: Plausible deniability  
Primary risks: coercion, inspection

---

## Emergency

Focus: Delivery under censorship  
Primary risks: network collapse

---

## Notes

Threat models are guides, not guarantees.
Users should understand their own risks.
